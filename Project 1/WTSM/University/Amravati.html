About SGBAU

Sant Gadge Baba Amravati University (SGBAU) was established in 1983. It offers higher education in various fields such as Arts, Commerce, Science, Engineering, and Management, serving students from the Vidarbha region of Maharashtra.

Colleges Under SGBAU

  • Diploma Colleges: 50+ affiliated diploma colleges
  • Engineering Colleges: 60+ affiliated engineering colleges
  • Management Institutes: 40+ MBA institutes
  • Science and Arts Colleges: 200+ affiliated colleges

Placement Statistics

  • Average Placement: 55-70% across various programs
  • Top Recruiters: Infosys, TCS, Capgemini, Cognizant, Wipro
  • Highest Package: ₹10 LPA
  • Average Package: ₹3-4 LPA
