Savitribai Phule Pune University (SPPU)

About SPPU

SPPU, also known as Pune University, was established in 1949. It is one of the premier universities in India, offering a wide range of UG, PG, and Doctoral programs.

Colleges Under SPPU

  • Diploma Colleges: 100+ affiliated diploma colleges
  • Engineering Colleges: 150+ affiliated engineering colleges
  • Management Institutes: 50+ MBA institutes
  • Science and Arts Colleges: 200+ affiliated colleges

Placement Statistics

  • Average Placement: 70-85% across various programs
  • Top Recruiters: TCS, Infosys, Wipro, Accenture, Cognizant
  • Highest Package: ₹25 LPA (in top institutes under SPPU)
  • Average Package: ₹4-6 LPA (across different domains)
