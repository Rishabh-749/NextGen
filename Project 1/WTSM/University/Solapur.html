About PAHSU

Punyashlok Ahilyadevi Holkar Solapur University was established in 2004. It offers education in various fields such as Arts, Commerce, Science, Engineering, and Management, serving students across the Solapur region.

Colleges Under PAHSU

  • Diploma Colleges: 35+ affiliated diploma colleges
  • Engineering Colleges: 45+ affiliated engineering colleges
  • Management Institutes: 25+ MBA institutes
  • Science and Arts Colleges: 120+ affiliated colleges

Placement Statistics

  • Average Placement: 55-70% across various programs
  • Top Recruiters: Infosys, Wipro, TCS, Accenture, Capgemini
  • Highest Package: ₹10 LPA
  • Average Package: ₹3.5-4.5 LPA
