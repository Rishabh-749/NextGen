About SRTMU

Swami Ramanand Teerth Marathwada University (SRTMU) was established in 1994. It provides education in various fields such as Arts, Commerce, Science, Engineering, and Management, focusing on the educational needs of students from the Marathwada region.

Colleges Under SRTMU

  • Diploma Colleges: 40+ affiliated diploma colleges
  • Engineering Colleges: 50+ affiliated engineering colleges
  • Management Institutes: 30+ MBA institutes
  • Science and Arts Colleges: 150+ affiliated colleges

Placement Statistics

  • Average Placement: 50-65% across various programs
  • Top Recruiters: Infosys, Wipro, TCS, Cognizant, Capgemini
  • Highest Package: ₹9 LPA
  • Average Package: ₹3-4 LPA
