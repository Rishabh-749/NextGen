Rashtrasant Tukadoji Maharaj Nagpur University (RTMNU)

About RTMNU

Rashtrasant Tukadoji Maharaj Nagpur University, formerly known as Nagpur University, was established in 1923. It is a well-renowned university offering UG, PG, and doctoral programs.

Colleges Under RTMNU

  • Diploma Colleges: 90+ affiliated diploma colleges
  • Engineering Colleges: 120+ affiliated engineering colleges
  • Management Institutes: 60+ MBA institutes
  • Science and Arts Colleges: 250+ affiliated colleges

Placement Statistics

  • Average Placement: 60-75% across various programs
  • Top Recruiters: TCS, Infosys, HCL, Tech Mahindra, Wipro
  • Highest Package: ₹20 LPA (in top institutes under RTMNU)
  • Average Package: ₹3-6 LPA (across different domains)
