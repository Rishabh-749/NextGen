Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Technological University (DBATU)

About DBATU

Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Technological University (DBATU) is a state technological university in Maharashtra, established in 1989. It offers various UG, PG, and doctoral programs in engineering and technology.

Colleges Under DBATU

  • Diploma Colleges: 80+ affiliated diploma colleges
  • Engineering Colleges: 100+ affiliated engineering colleges
  • Management Institutes: 50+ MBA institutes
  • Pharmacy Colleges: 70+ affiliated colleges

Placement Statistics

  • Average Placement: 65-80% across various programs
  • Top Recruiters: TCS, Infosys, L&T, Wipro, Capgemini
  • Highest Package: ₹18 LPA (in top institutes under DBATU)
  • Average Package: ₹3-7 LPA (across different domains)
