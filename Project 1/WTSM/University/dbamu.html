About BAMU

Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Marathwada University (BAMU) was established in 1958. It is one of the premier institutions in Maharashtra, providing quality education in various fields including Arts, Science, Commerce, Engineering, and Management.

Colleges Under BAMU

  • Diploma Colleges: 60+ affiliated diploma colleges
  • Engineering Colleges: 70+ affiliated engineering colleges
  • Management Institutes: 50+ MBA institutes
  • Science and Arts Colleges: 250+ affiliated colleges

Placement Statistics

  • Average Placement: 60-75% across various programs
  • Top Recruiters: Infosys, TCS, Wipro, HCL, Accenture
  • Highest Package: ₹12 LPA
  • Average Package: ₹3-4.5 LPA
