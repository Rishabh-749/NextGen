About University of Mumbai

The University of Mumbai, formerly known as Bombay University, was established in 1857. It is one of the oldest universities in India, offering diverse programs in various fields.

Colleges Under University of Mumbai

  • Diploma Colleges: 80+ affiliated diploma colleges
  • Engineering Colleges: 100+ affiliated engineering colleges
  • Management Institutes: 70+ MBA institutes
  • Science and Arts Colleges: 300+ affiliated colleges

Placement Statistics

  • Average Placement: 65-80% across various programs
  • Top Recruiters: TCS, Infosys, L&T, Capgemini, IBM
  • Highest Package: ₹30 LPA (in top institutes under Mumbai University)
  • Average Package: ₹5-7 LPA (across different domains)
