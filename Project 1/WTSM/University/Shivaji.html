Shivaji University, Kolhapur

About Shivaji University

Shivaji University was established in 1962 and is named after Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj. It offers a variety of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across multiple disciplines.

Colleges Under Shivaji University

  • Diploma Colleges: 50+ affiliated diploma colleges
  • Engineering Colleges: 80+ affiliated engineering colleges
  • Management Institutes: 40+ MBA institutes
  • Science and Arts Colleges: 200+ affiliated colleges

Placement Statistics

  • Average Placement: 65-80% across various programs
  • Top Recruiters: Infosys, Cognizant, Wipro, TCS, Capgemini
  • Highest Package: ₹15 LPA
  • Average Package: ₹3-5 LPA
