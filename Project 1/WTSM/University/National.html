Visvesvaraya National Institute of Technology (VNIT), Nagpur

About VNIT

Visvesvaraya National Institute of Technology (VNIT), formerly known as VRCE, is one of India's premier engineering institutions. Established in 1960, it offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in engineering, sciences, and architecture.

Academic Programs

  • B.Tech Programs: Civil, Mechanical, Electrical, Electronics, Computer Science, and more.
  • M.Tech Programs: Specializations in various engineering disciplines.
  • Ph.D. Programs: Research opportunities in multiple fields.

Placement Statistics

  • Average Placement: 80-90% across various programs
  • Top Recruiters: Google, Microsoft, Amazon, TCS, Infosys
  • Highest Package: ₹40 LPA
  • Average Package: ₹10-12 LPA
