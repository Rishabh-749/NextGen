Scholarship & Financial Aid Information

1. Government Scholarships

  • Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj Scholarship
  • Post Matric Scholarship for OBC, SC, ST Students
  • Dr. Panjabrao Deshmukh Hostel Maintenance Allowance
  • TFWS (Tuition Fee Waiver Scheme)

2. Private Scholarships

  • Tata Trust Scholarship
  • L&T Build India Scholarship
  • Jindal Foundation Scholarship
  • Reliance Foundation Scholarship

3. Merit-Based & Need-Based Scholarships

Students can apply for scholarships based on academic performance and financial background.

  • Merit-based: Based on MHT-CET/JEE scores.
  • Need-based: For students from economically weaker sections.

4. Application Process

Follow these steps to apply for scholarships:

  • Check eligibility criteria.
  • Register on the MahaDBT Portal.
  • Fill the application form and upload necessary documents.
  • Submit and track application status.
