Engineering Admission Process Guide

1. Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for engineering admissions in Maharashtra, students must:

  • Pass 12th standard with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics (PCM) with minimum marks as per category.
  • Appear for MHT-CET or JEE Main (for some private colleges).

2. Admission Process Steps

The admission process follows these steps:

  • Step 1: Register on the DTE Maharashtra website.
  • Step 2: Fill application form & upload required documents.
  • Step 3: Document verification at Facilitation Centers.
  • Step 4: Check merit list & cutoff.
  • Step 5: Participate in CAP Rounds (Centralized Admission Process).
  • Step 6: Seat allocation & reporting to allotted college.

3. Documents Required

  • 10th & 12th Marksheet
  • CET/JEE Scorecard
  • School Leaving Certificate
  • Domicile & Caste Certificate (if applicable)
  • Income Certificate (for scholarships & TFWS quota)
